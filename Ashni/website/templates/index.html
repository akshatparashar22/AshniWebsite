Welcome to Ashni Foundation

ASHNI Foundation is a PAN India not for profit organization working towards building consciousness and awareness about Gender Sensitivity, Prevention of Sexual Harassment at work, Women’s and Children’s rights towards Education and Health. Foundation majorly works in building network of resources for trainers / facilitators, counselors and external members who contribute to make workplaces gender-sensitive and safe places for professional development. ASHNI has been working for last six years informally as a group of Social Workers, Counselors, Psychologists, HR and Labor law practitioners and our members have been working as experts in the field of POSH, Prevention of Sexual Harassment at workplaces, ECCE Staff Capacity Building, Life Skills / Soft Skills, supporting NGOs to develop and assess development delay. We also provide soft skills training and consultancy to other charity organizations to build robust systems of HR practices, processes and SOPs and project management. During lockdown, we realized the need of developing ourself as a resource organization and expand our programs hence applied for registration as a society on 05-Aug-21 under registration no Maha/838/2021/Pune and registered as a Public Trust on 02-Jun-22 under registration no F-58092 for Pune Region.

ASHNI's Projects

Sath Sath – Building Resources and Supporting Networks and Organisations

  • Counsellors’ Support Network: ASHNI has been instrumental in building the networking platforms using common and easily available networking resources. Members of ASHNI supported a support group –Counsellors and Allied Professionals network.
  • PoSH IC External Members: A vibrant network of professionals working in the field of Prevention of Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace has been initiated by ASHNI members and also being managed and supported by sharing of knowledge resources.
  • Parenting Sessions in collaboration with New Vision: In year 2021-22 ASHNI’s members conducted staff capacity building session for New Vision project staff for Early Childhood Care and Education program. ASHNI organised Two half days ToT workshops for ASHNI’s members and resources persons in ASHNI office. Post attending ToT, three members and three resource persons specially trained by ASHNI’s ECCD Expert conducted sessions in New Vision’s Centres at the construction sites for their beneficiaries in 11 communities. These interactive sessions attended by both male and female parents. Children were engaged in interesting activities while their parents participated in the parenting sessions. Every community received two sessions and inputs on role of parents in growth and development of children, diet and nutrition and how it impacts various domains of development. ASHNI also distributed the pictorial resource material specially designed for these sessions for the future reference of New Vision Teachers and parents.
  • Supporting other NGOs: ASHNI helped Shishuadhar for child in developing HR Policies, Whistle Blower policy and SOPs and Policy on Preventing Sexual Exploitation and Abuse (PSEA) which is a mandate for all the organisations working as partner organisation for UNICEF.
  • Youth Empowerment: ASHNI conducted an online webinar on Gender Sensitization where in emphasis was given in more gender friendly spaces and how to have a gender just attitude and culture of respect for other genders at Tilak Maharashtra Vidaypeeth’s Institute of Hotel Management. There was one in person workshop on Stress Management was organised and resources person from ASHNI facilitated for the for the students of Hotel Management studying at Tilak Maharashtra Vidyapeeth.


PoSH – Prevention of Sexual Harassment
ASHNI came in existence due to increasing demand of awareness sessions for employees as a statutory compliance with the Implementation of the Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace Act- 2013 in various organisations. Members of ASHNI Subject Matter Experts for this domain and were working individually as per their individual capacity and interests in PoSH. ASHNI introduced one of its vertical as providing one stop services for all those organisations who are interested in developing a preventive and responsive framework for curbing the sexual harassment of women at workplace. Currently ASHNI is supporting its partner organisations – two NGOs and 15 corporates. Following services are provided by ASHNI
  • Assistance in Formation of internal committee and designing robust Anti-Sexual Harassment policies.
  • Conducting gender sensitivity workshops and awareness programs for employees.
  • Orientation and capacity building of Internal Committee Members.
  • Empanelment of External Member on the Internal Committee
In 2021-22 members of ASHNI Foundation helped its partner organisations to complete their legal compliance by facilitating the PoSH sessions. From August 2021 to March 22 ASHNI conducted around 12 Awareness Sessions for employees which includes information on major provisions of the act, gender sensitivity and complaint handling procedures. ASHNI also conducted 12 sessions for the Internal Committee members in the partner organisations and helped them to understand the prevention, prohibition and redressal expected in the act and helped them to get clarity on role and responsibility of Internal Committee Members.

Jeevan-Shala – Life Skills Education
Juvenile Justice Board approached various NGOs working for children and Youth for imparting Vocational Guidance and other skills training to inmates at Special Home and Observation Home at Yerawada. ASHNI offered to conduct counseling sessions and Life skills sessions for Juveniles who are in conflict with law at Pune. In the Jeevan Shala Project of ASHNI, members of ASHNI facilitated Life Skills Sessions for Juveniles at Special Home and Observation home at Pandit Jawahalal Nehru Udyog Kendra on fortnightly basis ie on every first and third Saturday of every month. In year 2021-22 ASHNI conducted 9 sessions which were appreciated by the inmates and staff.
